Biography

Dr. Subhajit Chakraborty is a researcher specializing in photonics science and engineering. He completed his doctoral studies through a prestigious joint PhD program between the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur) and The University of Melbourne. This cross-institutional background has shaped his interdisciplinary approach to physical sciences and modern engineering.

His core expertise lies at the intersection of optical imaging architectures and precise physical instrumentation. With a strong footing in both experimental photonics and computational frameworks, Dr. Chakraborty builds and refines optical systems tailored for fine-grained microscopy, spectroscopic investigation, and non-destructive optical metrology.

Driven by curiosity about how computation can break traditional physical boundaries in hardware, his emerging research directions actively target vision-based automated analysis, computer vision algorithms, and intelligent, software-defined imaging nodes.
