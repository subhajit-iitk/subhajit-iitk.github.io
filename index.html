Biography

Dr. Subhajit Chakraborty is a researcher in photonics science and engineering. He completed his doctoral studies through a prestigious joint PhD program between the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur) and The University of Melbourne. This cross-institutional background has shaped his interdisciplinary approach to physical sciences and modern engineering.

His core expertise lies at the intersection of optical imaging architectures and precise physical instrumentation. With a strong footing in both experimental photonics and computational frameworks, Dr. Chakraborty builds and refines optical systems tailored for fine-grained microscopy, spectroscopic investigation, and non-destructive optical metrology.

Driven by curiosity about how computation can break traditional physical boundaries in hardware, his emerging research directions actively target vision-based automated analysis, computer vision algorithms, and intelligent, software-defined imaging nodes.

Research Interests

Optical Imaging

Advanced imaging methodologies for visualization and characterization of microscopic and nanoscale objects.

Microscopy

Development of optical microscopy techniques for enhanced contrast, resolution, and quantitative analysis.

Spectroscopy

Photothermal and thermal lens spectroscopy for studying molecular interactions and material properties.

Optical Metrology

Precision optical measurement and characterization methods validating physical models.

Computational Imaging

Combining optics, algorithms, and computation to extract structural information from imaging systems.

Image Processing

Signal and image analysis using statistical pipelines and optimization approaches.

Computer Vision

Emerging interests in vision-based data analysis and intelligent, adaptive imaging systems.

Research Highlights

High-Refractive-Index Nanoparticle Imaging

Developed novel optical methodologies for imaging and characterization of high-refractive-index nanoparticles using brightfield microscopy layouts.

Thermal Lens Spectroscopy

Investigated synergistic molecular interactions in binary liquid mixtures using custom thermal lens spectroscopic measurements.

Advanced Optical Instrumentation

Designed and built rugged optical systems alongside dedicated experimental platforms tailored for challenging imaging and spectroscopy applications.
